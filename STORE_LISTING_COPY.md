**Promotional text**

**Keywords**  
church,worship,sermon,ministry,leader,follower,congregation,session,speech,library,faith

**Description**  
Prayer Companion helps prayer leaders prepare content and guide a group through a synchronized prayer session.

A leader can organize a prayer library, prepare sermons or speeches, select the active prayer, and present the session. Followers can join and see the prayer selected by the leader on their own device.

FOR PRAYER LEADERS
• Create and organize a reusable prayer library
• Prepare sermons, speeches, and prayer-session content
• Select the active prayer for the group
• Lead sessions from a phone, tablet, computer, or supported display
• Keep session content organized for future use

FOR FOLLOWERS
• Join the leader’s session
• Follow the currently selected prayer
• Use a clear, focused display during group prayer
• Stay synchronized as the leader moves through the session

FOR MINISTRIES AND GROUPS
• Support organized prayer meetings
• Reduce the need for printed copies
• Help participants follow the same content
• Prepare reusable material for future gatherings

Prayer Companion provides tools for organizing and presenting user-created religious content. Available features may depend on account role and plan.
