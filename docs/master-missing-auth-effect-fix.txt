MASTER MISSING AUTH EFFECT FIX

Changed:
- app/master/page.tsx

What was wrong:
- authReady state and "Checking login..." guard existed
- but the subscribeToAuth useEffect was missing
- result: /master stayed forever on "Checking login..."

What this patch adds:
- subscribeToAuth listener near the top of MasterPage
- redirect to /auth when logged out
- setCurrentUserEmail when logged in
- setAuthReady(true) immediately after auth is confirmed
- fetch profile in background and populate currentUserProfile
- console error if profile load fails
